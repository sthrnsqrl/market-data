2025 Ohio Festival Guide

Your festival event guide for Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Dayton, Marietta, Toledo, Youngstown and all of Ohio.

Welcome to Ohio’s most comprehensive festival listing, where you’ll find festivals and things to do in such cities as Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Toledo, Zanesville, Youngstown, Dayton and everywhere in-between. I’m constantly working hard to make this the state’s best collection of festivals.

If you want festival listings for specific areas of Ohio, I currently have listings for Cleveland, Cincinnati and Columbus.

Before traveling to any festivals, please be sure to double check events via the link provided as events could have been rescheduled or cancelled without my knowledge, especially after damaging weather. If there is an * (asterisk) next to the date, this means that I have not confirmed the date.

“My Review” means that it’s a link to my review of that festival.

If there is a festival you do not see but would like listed, please contact me and let me know about it, especially smaller festivals that don’t have the advertising.

Please note that craft shows, machinery shows, bazaars, county fairs and some other events can be found on the Fairs, Shows, Expos, Bazaars Page.
